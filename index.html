The iconic Singapore Zoo and Jurong Bird Park have united to form the Singapore Wildlife Foundation (SWF). This merger creates Southeast Asia's largest wildlife sanctuary, combining the Zoo's renowned animal habitats with Jurong's world-class aviaries. Visitors can now access both locations with a single pass, while the foundation focuses on enhanced conservation and research initiatives across the region.
